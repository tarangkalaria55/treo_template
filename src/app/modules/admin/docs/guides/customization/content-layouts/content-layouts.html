Content Layouts

Like Theme layouts, Treo also provides examples for variety of different content layouts. These layouts are designed for the content of the app and they provide consistency.

Unlike Theme layouts, Content layouts are NOT components, they are basically pre-made pages for you to reference and use it as the shell for your pages.
